Layout: aside (sidenote)

Asides are sidenotes that float to the right of the main column on wide viewports and flow inline below 1030px. On a narrow window, this paragraph and the aside box stack vertically — try resizing the page.

Sidenote. The aside uses position: absolute at large widths and inline flow on small ones. Wrap it in .layout-aside-host so it anchors to the right place.

On large screens the aside lives in the negative-space margin to the right of the main column, similar to footnotes in Tufte-style typography or marginalia in academic prose.
